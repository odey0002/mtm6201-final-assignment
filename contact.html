Contact Information
and Hours

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions about adoption, fostering, volunteering, or anything else, we're here to help. You can reach us via email at info@pawsitivefutures.org or call us at (555) 123-4567. Our team is available to assist you during our business hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM, and Saturday, 10 AM – 4 PM. If you'd like to visit us in person, check out our location on the map below. We can't wait to connect with you!

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Pawsitive Futures Animal Rescue is located at 123 Hope Lane, Ottawa, ON 14201, in a friendly and accessible area. It's just a 10-minute drive from downtown Ottawa, with plenty of parking available on-site. If you're taking public transportation, the Brighton Community Bus Line stops just a short walk away at Hope Lane and Main Street. Look for our bright, welcoming sign at the entrance. It's easy to spot and a warm reminder that you've arrived at a place where every paw matters.
